# What is Traccio?

Traccio is a job-hunt tracking application. It allows you to organize your job search, and keep track of applications you submitted using a simple and effective platform. No more lifeless text-files and spreadsheets. A job search can be soul-draining as it is, maybe Traccio can help make it just a bit more bareable?

Traccio also allows you to keep track of your job-hunt progress. Take note of when you're applying less frequently than you'd like, or how often have you heard back from companies! Traccio is meant to help you apply smart! No more applying to the same position twice (or 15 times), always have a clear vision of where you are in the process. Log in your application, and let Traccio handle the rest.

# Features

  • Organize job hunts using Journeys. Separate them however convenient, and better visualize trends in your application processes.

  • Log different details about a job application. Is your application still in review? Have you completed an online assessment? What documents did you submit? Log in and modify all these details, so they're easily available when, say, you're called in for an interview!

  • Visualize your application progress in a given Journey. Your dashboard is the heart and soul of Traccio, use it to keep yourself motivated and constantly re-asses where you are in the process.

# Get Started

Sign up for a Traccio account today!

Use Traccio conveniently on mobile by adding it to your homescreen:

  • On iOS:
    • •   Launch Safari, and go to traccio.app
    • •   Tap the Share icon (a square with an arrow pointing out) at the bottom of the screen
    • •   Select Add to Home Screen from the list of actions
  • On Android:
    • •   Launch the Chrome app, and go to traccio.app
    • •   Tap the menu icon (three vertical dots icon) at the upper right-hand corner
    • •   Select Add to homescreen

# Support & Feature Requests

Traccio is an open source web application. For feature requests, bug reporting, or support, feel free to use any of the options provided below.

  • Feature requests:
    • •   Email your feature request to support@traccio.app. Do try to use "Feature Request" in your subject line.
    • •   Open an issue in the app repository. However, be sure to check the app roadmap first for planned features, your request might already be a planned development!
  • Support:
    • •   If you're facing a specific issue with the app, email your support requests to support@traccio.app. Be sure to include any screenshots that might aid in resolving your issue. Add "Support Request" to your subject line.
  • Bug reports:
    • •   Email your bug reports to support@traccio.app. Be sure to include any screenshots that might aid in resolving your issue. Add "Bug Report" to your subject line.
    • •   Open an issue in the app repository. Be sure to include any screenshots or reproduction steps to help with the bug squashing!
